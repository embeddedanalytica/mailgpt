FAQ

Is this a training plan app?

It’s coaching-first. You check in, it responds with what’s next. Plans exist, but they’re meant to adapt.

Do I need Strava or Garmin?

No. You can use it with simple check-ins. Optional integrations may enhance the experience later.

What sports is it for?

Starting with endurance-style training (running/cycling), expanding from there.

Is this replacing a human coach?

It’s designed for people who can’t or don’t want the overhead of traditional coaching. Coaches will also be able to use it later.
