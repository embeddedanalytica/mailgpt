## General Reading Recommendations

Outlive (Peter Attia): A framework for thinking about exercise as medicine for the decades ahead — not just race performance but long-term healthspan. Recommend when the athlete expresses interest in longevity, overall health beyond sport performance, or asks about the bigger picture of why they train.

Endure (Alex Hutchinson): What actually determines when you have to stop, and how much of it is in your head. Covers the science of human limits, motivation, and race-day psychology. Recommend when the athlete is curious about the mental side of endurance, struggles with race-day confidence, or wants to understand their own limits better.

Peak Performance (Brad Stulberg & Steve Magness): The stress-rest-growth cycle applied to both athletic and professional performance. Recommend when the athlete is balancing training with a demanding career or struggling with the concept that rest is productive.
