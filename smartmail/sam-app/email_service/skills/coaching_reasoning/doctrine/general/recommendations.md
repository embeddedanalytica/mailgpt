## General Reading Recommendations

## Rules
- Outlive (Peter Attia): recommend when athlete expresses interest in longevity, overall health beyond sport performance, or the bigger picture of why they train
- Endure (Alex Hutchinson): recommend when athlete is curious about the mental side of endurance, struggles with race-day confidence, or wants to understand their own limits
- Peak Performance (Brad Stulberg & Steve Magness): recommend when athlete is balancing training with a demanding career or struggling with the concept that rest is productive
