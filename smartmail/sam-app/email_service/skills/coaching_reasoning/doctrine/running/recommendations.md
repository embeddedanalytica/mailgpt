## Running Reading Recommendations

Daniels' Running Formula (Jack Daniels): The gold standard for understanding training zones and why easy running matters as much as hard sessions. Best for athletes who want to understand the science behind their plan. Recommend when the athlete asks about pacing, training zones, or wants to understand why easy days are so slow.

Advanced Marathoning (Pete Pfitzinger & Scott Douglas): The best bridge between general running fitness and race-specific marathon or half-marathon preparation. Structured plans with clear physiological rationale. Recommend when the athlete is preparing for a specific race distance and wants to understand periodization in a running context.

80/20 Running (Matt Fitzgerald): The science behind why running easy most of the time makes you faster on race day. Excellent for athletes who struggle with pace discipline on easy days or who feel guilty about running slowly. Recommend when the athlete's easy runs are consistently too fast or they express frustration about "slow" paces.
