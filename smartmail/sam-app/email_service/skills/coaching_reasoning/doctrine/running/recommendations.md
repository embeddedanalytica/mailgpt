## Running Reading Recommendations

## Rules
- Daniels' Running Formula (Jack Daniels): recommend when athlete asks about pacing, training zones, or wants to understand why easy days are so slow
- Advanced Marathoning (Pete Pfitzinger & Scott Douglas): recommend when athlete is preparing for a specific race distance and wants to understand periodization in a running context
- 80/20 Running (Matt Fitzgerald): recommend when athlete's easy runs are consistently too fast or they express frustration about "slow" paces
